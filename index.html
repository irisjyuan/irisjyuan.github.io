ABOUT ME

I am currently a freshman studying Computer Science at Purdue University, originally from Palo Alto, CA. My passion for coding began in high school with my first hackathon I attended, and since then I’ve loved the creativity involved in building apps and programs. I’m a mentor for Purdue University's MAGIC program, Mentoring Aspiring Girls In Computing, where we teach middle and high school girls simple web development and design each week and help them find their enthusiasm for coding.
